Our Team

David Schroeder

Founder

David is a student in ITP303 and also the founder of this restaurant. He wanted to create a restaurant because he has a passion for cooking, and he wanted to make one that had a menu that is constantly changing.

Gordon Ramsay

Executive Chef

Gordon is a world-famous chef known for his television presence on shows like Kitchen Nightmares and Hell's Kitchen. He was presented the idea of EatLA and was instantly intrigued and wanted to be involved, and is now in charge of picking the guest chefs and rotating menus.

Mindy Kaling

Restaurant Manager

Mindy is an actress known for her work on the big and small screen in films like Late Night and Inside Out and is a long-time friend of David's. She is in charge of managing the restaurant and the staff.
